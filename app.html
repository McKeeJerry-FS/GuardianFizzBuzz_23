FizzBuzz: A JavaScript Coding Challenge

Utilizing loos and the power of JavaScript, we display the numbers 1 thru 100. For every number that is divisible by three, the word Fizz is displayed. For every number that is divisible by 5, the word Buzz is displayed. If a displayed number is divisible by both 3 and 5, FizzBuzz is displayed.
